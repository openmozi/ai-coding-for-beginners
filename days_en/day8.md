# Chapter 8: Automated UI and App Building

## Course Overview

> "Prompt your way to a fully fledged app"

AI democratizes app building. Frontend development threshold greatly reduced. Complex apps still need engineer judgment.

---

## 1. Web Development Evolution

### Old Era
- LAMP, MERN, JAM

### First Generation Low-code
- Wix, Squarespace, Webflow
- Limited customization

### AI New Era
- Lovable, Replit, v0, Base44
- Natural language generates complete apps

---

## 2. What AI Changed?

### Engineers becoming more cross-functional
- **Design** skills
- **Product management** skills

### Anyone can build apps
- More user-friendly than first generation
